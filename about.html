Who is he on the righttop?

Well that is Siyabonga Mtshali , The director and founder of Brain Space(Pty)Ltd. Brought up in Heidelberg, Siyabonga believes that pictures are more than just grids of pixels but rather memories and art, and that creativity should have no limitations.

It all started during his high school years when he held a camera in his hands for the first time, the initial moment where the photography and design interest and passion ignited, his first response was to pick up a few books and enroll at an institution, and with the discovery of the niche he started researching, studying and practising which eventually led to him being equipped with the right skills and Brain Space was born.

Our inspiration has always been to capture the essence of memories, telling stories and a reflection art. Since established Brain Space has known for an unparalleled commitment to customer sastifaction, it is this level of excellence that has provided the impetus for us to grow into the business we are today.
